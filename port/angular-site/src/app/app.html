Reckless Drivin' — WebAssembly Port

A fan-made SDL2 / Emscripten port of the classic Mac OS 9 racing game Reckless Drivin' by Jonas Echterhoff. The original source was released by the creator at github.com/jechter/RecklessDrivin . When clicking Start, hold the number key for the level you want to jump to in order to skip directly there. This effort builds on earlier ports such as Nathan Craddock’s work and Pomme by Jorio.
